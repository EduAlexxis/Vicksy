Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

At Vicksy, we believe your academic data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Vicksy is designed to be private by default. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties.

  • Local Storage: Your classes, homework, and grades are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, your data is synced securely using Apple's CloudKit. We (the developers) do not have access to this data; it is encrypted by Apple and accessible only by you.

2. Notifications

We use local notifications to remind you of upcoming classes and due assignments. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent to any external server.

3. No Tracking

Vicksy does not use any third-party analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking cookies. You are not being tracked.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@vicksyapp.com.
